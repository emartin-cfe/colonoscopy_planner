Preparing for your colonoscopy

Why is it Important to Get Cleaned Inside?

Your doctor must be able to see the inside of the colon clearly, in order to do the test right. If it is dirty on the inside, your doctor may not be able to see important things, like polyps or cancer, and may even have to do the test again. That would mean you would have to start over, and nobody wants that. So help us help you make this "one and done."

Imagine This

Think of it this way: a clean colon is like driving on a country road on a sunny day. A dirty colon is like driving in a snowstorm.



When your colon is clean, doing the colonoscopy is like driving on a country road on a sunny day. It is easy to see and to drive.




When your colon is dirty, doing the colonoscopy is like driving on a winter road in a snow storm. It is hard to see and hard to drive.
